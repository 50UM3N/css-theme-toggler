Alligator.io is joining the DigitalOcean Community!

I started Alligator.io a few years ago as a way to help me learn and share my insights with the community. At the time, I had no idea it would grow to become the platform it is today, and I couldn’t be prouder.

As the platform grew, it welcomed contributors from all backgrounds to publish about what they know and love with the community.

Always with a deep love and appreciation for the web platform and the JavaScript ecosystem, Alligator.io has now grown to be a major source of knowledge for anybody who' s interested in modern web development.

I’m excited to announce that starting in May, Alligator.io will be joining the DigitalOcean Community.

While the topics covered within each of our sites are different, we share a passion for technical writing and a common goal of helping developers build, solve problems, and learn.
